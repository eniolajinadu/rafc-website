About Rooney Athletic Football Club

Our History

Founded in 1990, Rooney Athletic Football Club has grown from a small local team to one of the most successful clubs in the country. Our journey has been marked by passion, dedication, and a commitment to excellence both on and off the field.

With 10 championship titles and numerous cup victories, RAFC has established itself as a powerhouse in American football. Our success is built on a foundation of talented players, visionary coaches, and unwavering support from our fans.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in every aspect of our club.
  • Integrity: We conduct ourselves with honesty and respect, both on and off the field.
  • Community: We are committed to making a positive impact in our local community.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technologies to stay at the forefront of the sport.

Our Facilities

Rooney Stadium: Our state-of-the-art home ground with a capacity of 60,000 fans.

RAFC Training Complex: A cutting-edge facility for player development and preparation.
